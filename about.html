About Neyveli

Discover the historical, administrative, and cultural profile of the industrial township

History of Neyveli

Neyveli is a major industrial city in the Cuddalore district of Tamil Nadu. Its history is deeply intertwined with the discovery of lignite resources in the region, which paved the way for the establishment of NLC India Limited (formerly Neyveli Lignite Corporation) in 1956. This transformation turned a small group of villages into a bustling, well-planned township.

Culture and Heritage

The culture of Neyveli is a beautiful blend of traditional Tamil heritage and a cosmopolitan lifestyle brought by people from various parts of India who settled here for employment. Festivals are celebrated with great fervor, reflecting the unity in diversity among the township's residents.

Importance of Neyveli

Neyveli holds significant importance for the state and the nation as a crucial hub for power generation. It helps fulfill a large portion of South India's electricity demands, acting as a backbone for industrial and domestic energy needs.

Development and Growth

Over the decades, Neyveli has seen exponential development in terms of infrastructure, education, and healthcare. The township boasts excellent schools, modern hospitals, and well-maintained roads, making it one of the most organized and livable cities in the region.

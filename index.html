About Neyveli

Neyveli is a major industrial city in the Cuddalore district of Tamil Nadu. Known as the "Power City", it is the home of NLC India Limited, a massive lignite mining and thermal power generation enterprise. Beyond its industrial prowess, Neyveli is a beautifully planned township featuring lush green parks, renowned educational institutions, robust healthcare, and a rich cultural landscape.

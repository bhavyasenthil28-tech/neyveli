Sri Golden Vinayagar Temple

The Golden Vinayagar Temple is a well-maintained modern shrine in Block 24 of the Neyveli Township. Known for its quiet and peaceful surroundings, the temple attracts local residents for daily prayers. The deity of Lord Vinayagar (Ganesha) is beautifully decorated during festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi. The temple also features shrines for Navagrahas and Lord Murugan.

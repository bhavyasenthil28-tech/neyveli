Chidambaram Nataraja Temple

Chidambaram Nataraja Temple, also referred to as the Thillai Nataraja Temple, is a Hindu temple dedicated to Nataraja—Shiva as the lord of dance—located in Chidambaram, Tamil Nadu. It is one of the most celebrated shrines in South India, famed for its golden roofed sanctum and profound spiritual significance.

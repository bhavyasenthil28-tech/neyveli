Local Auto Stand & Taxi Services

The Block 1 Auto Rickshaw and Taxi Stand is the most reliable place to hire local transport within the Neyveli Township. Auto-rickshaws are available for transit to all blocks, hospitals, and schools. For outstation travel, several private taxi operators run hatchbacks, sedans, and SUVs on rent.

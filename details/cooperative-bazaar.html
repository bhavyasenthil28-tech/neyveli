Cooperative Bazaar

The Cooperative Bazaar is the primary municipal marketplace of Neyveli Township, situated in Block 11. It is a bustling hub that hosts dozens of retail shops under one roof, catering to the daily needs of township residents.

The bazaar features a wide variety of stores including clothing boutiques, hardware stores, grocery vendors, fruit and vegetable stalls, and textile shops. Known for its vibrant atmosphere and affordable prices, the Cooperative Bazaar is a cornerstone of Neyveli's local commerce and community life.

Neyveli Post Office

The Head Post Office of Neyveli, located in Block 2, is the primary postal hub for the township. It offers a complete suite of services, including speed post, registered mail, parcel booking, money orders, and passport application services. It also manages postal savings accounts, public provident fund (PPF) schemes, and acts as the delivery base for all township blocks.

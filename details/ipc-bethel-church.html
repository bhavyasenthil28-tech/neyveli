IPC Neyveli Bethel Church

The IPC Neyveli Bethel Church is a vibrant congregation belonging to the India Pentecostal Church of God (IPC). Located in Block 5, this church is known for its spirit-filled worship services, prayer meetings, and evangelical programs. It draws families from all blocks of Neyveli who participate in weekly cottage prayers and Bible study sessions.

EB Office – TANGEDCO / TNEB

The Tamil Nadu Generation and Distribution Corporation (TANGEDCO), formerly known as the Tamil Nadu Electricity Board (TNEB), operates a key divisional office serving the Neyveli and Mandharakuppam areas. This EB Office is the primary point of contact for all electricity-related services for residents and businesses in the region.

Services provided include new electricity connection applications, disconnection and reconnection requests, electricity bill collection (manual and counter-based), load assessment, lineman maintenance requests, and reporting of power failures or hazardous lines. The office also handles applications for domestic High Tension (HT) and Low Tension (LT) connections for commercial establishments in the area.

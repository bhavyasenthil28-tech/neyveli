Jawahar Matriculation Higher Secondary School

Jawahar Matriculation Higher Secondary School is a legendary educational institution in the Neyveli Township. Managed by the Jawahar Education Society and patronized by NLC India Limited, the school provides academic education under Matriculation and CBSE curricula. Famed for its exceptional academic results, massive sports grounds, science laboratories, and high student placement rates in elite engineering and medical colleges, it stands as the pride of Neyveli.

NLC Girls High School

NLC Girls High School, situated in Block 11, is a premier state-board school focusing on girls' education. Managed with funding support from NLCIL, the school provides high quality education, free notebooks, uniforms, and mid-day meals to students, ensuring high enrollment and literacy rates among girls in Cuddalore district.

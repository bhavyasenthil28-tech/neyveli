Indira Nagar Mosque

The Indira Nagar Mosque is a cozy, community-run mosque located in the suburb of Indira Nagar, just outside the NLC township. It serves as a vital spiritual center for local families and laborers working in nearby private enterprises, hosting daily congregational prayers and community discussions.

Block 18 Shopping Complex

The Block 18 Shopping Complex is one of the most active commercial hubs within Neyveli Township. Located in the heart of Block 18's main market area, it brings together a diverse mix of specialty retail shops catering to residents' educational, technology, and daily needs.

The complex hosts bookstores stocking school and college textbooks, electronics showrooms featuring mobile phones and accessories, stationery shops, and xerox centres. It is also home to several small eateries and snack shops, making it a lively destination particularly for students and families.

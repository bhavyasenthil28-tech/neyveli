NLC Cooperative Medicals

The NLC Employees Co-operative Medical Store in Block 11 is a government-supported pharmacy. Designed to make healthcare affordable, it offers medicines at highly subsidised rates to NLC employee families and the general public. It keeps a comprehensive stock of critical life-saving medicines.

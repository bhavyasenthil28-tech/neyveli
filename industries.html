Industries & Economic Growth

NLC India Limited Overview

NLCIL (NLC India Limited) is a premier 'Navratna' Government of India enterprise under the Ministry of Coal. As the economic backbone of Neyveli, it operates massive opencast lignite mines and thermal power stations that feed the southern power grid. It is the heart of Neyveli's history, economy, and planned township structure.

Mining Operations

The core of Neyveli's industry lies in its massive open-cast lignite mines. These sprawling operational areas employ state-of-the-art giant bucket wheel excavators and continuous mining technologies to extract lignite, which forms the primary fuel for the adjacent thermal power plants.

Power Generation Facilities

Neyveli houses several large-scale thermal power stations that convert lignite into electricity. These power plants are crucial for maintaining a steady flow of energy across the southern grid, ensuring that both industrial and domestic needs in the region are consistently met.

Township Development

Driven by industrial success, Neyveli has developed into a robust ecosystem. The township is a model of modern urban planning, offering exceptional healthcare, top-tier educational institutions, and meticulously maintained infrastructure that elevates the living standards of its residents.

Logistics & Commercial Transport

The movement of goods is vital to Neyveli's industrial and economic success. Supported by a robust network of commercial transport, including specialized cargo trucks and logistics services, materials and finished products are efficiently distributed across the state and country, keeping the local economy thriving.
